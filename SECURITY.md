# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

If you discover a security issue in JankMark, please **do not** open a public
issue. Instead, report it privately:

- Use GitHub's [private vulnerability reporting](https://github.com/AbiMangalan/JankMark/security/advisories/new), or
- Contact the maintainer through the [SoC Benchmarks](https://www.youtube.com/@socbenchmarks) channel.

Please include steps to reproduce and the JankMark version. You will get a
response as soon as reasonably possible.

## Scope

JankMark runs locally on your PC and communicates with your own Android device
over ADB. It does not transmit your telemetry to any server. Reports about data
handling, the bundled adb tooling, or the installer are all in scope.
